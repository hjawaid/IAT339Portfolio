How might Messenger use AI to help families capture memories

The project that helped me land a Facebook Internship.

Project Deck
Role:
SOUND DESIGNER
Timeline:
APRIL 2019 (2 Weeks)

In the summer of 2018, I created a plan for myself to complete two projects over 3 months and show them to designers in different cities to get their feedback. This was the first project of that plan where I imagined how Facebook Messenger might use AI to capture key moments during video calls between family members.

Inspiration

Body text example: One of the most important facets in ensuring that user’s feel comfortable sharing their data with an app is giving them control. One simple way this was implemented is allowing the ability to turn the feature on and off. Furthermore, I explored how users may want to turn the feature on only for certain people.

Highlight Example: Moments like seeing your nephew walk for the first time, your cousin announcing her engagement, or even little moments like laughing along with your family.
HIGHLIGHTED ANNOTATION

Facebook at its core is about connecting to the people you care about. They are also investing heavily in to exploring applications in their AI teams. That’s why I explored how to merge these two together to improve the experience of a group of users who hold strong connections with each other.
