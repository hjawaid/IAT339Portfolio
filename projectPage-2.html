Allowing music producers to create a sonic moodboard

Designing an end to end experience for music producers.

Project Deck
Role:
SOLE PRODUCT DESIGNER
Timeline:
JUNE - AUGUST 2018 (2 Months)

In the summer of 2018, I took a semester off school to do a self started design bootcamp where I would complete projects and then take them to industry professionals for feedback. The second project I completed was creating a web app from scratch that would allow musicians to create a sonic moodboard.

The Problem

Music producers constantly have song ideas running through their heads. However it takes so long to turn those concepts in to actual music that they end up being lost.

Processes like setting up equipment and sound selection can make an idea be forgotten.

The Solution: Intend

A tool for producers that would let them create a mood board consisting of sounds and other elements to help depict the vision of musical ideas.

Video Prototype

Implementing Design Principles

Early on, I realized that with this project, I was putting my hand in so many different pots. It was starting to become overwhelming and I lacked a clear direction. So I created a list of design principles to help focus on what the product stood for, in turn, helping me make decisions.

Centralize and simplify the workflow

Producers go through many overly complicated and unnecessary means to get certain tasks done. We want them to have an experience where each step of their production process is more efficient.

I propose a simpler workflow that allows users to hand pick new sounds for projects and use them right away

Make the board easily mouldable

Every producer values components of their music differently and could want to use this board with different intents. We need to allow them the freedom to make a board personalized to their situation and liking.

Producers working on an album may make a less organized board to allow a wider range of ideas
Producers working on an song may make a very organized board to capture an idea

Balance freedom with guidance

Producers want to use this tool to help portray their vision and help them create music with a more focused direction. This should be as easy as possible for them. This means while they should be given a lot of freedom, they should not have to think hard about decisions not relating to making music (ex. design decisions).

Allowing users to upload multiple files types to communicate their idea how they see fit.

Conclusion
